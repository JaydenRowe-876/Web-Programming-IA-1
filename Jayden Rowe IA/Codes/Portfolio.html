AI Business Idea

I chose an AI-powered mental health app from the AI Businesses of 2025 blog. This app provides personalized support such as mood tracking, stress management techniques, and virtual counseling sessions. It could help students and young adults manage stress and anxiety effectively.

Ethical concerns include data privacy, the risk of replacing human therapists, and ensuring accurate assessments. My solution is to use AI as a supportive tool, maintain strict data security, and regularly update algorithms based on research.

An example of such an app is Wysa – an AI chatbot for mental health: https://www.wysa.io
